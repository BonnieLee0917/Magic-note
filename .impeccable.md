## Design Context

### Users
Magic Note is for knowledge workers, students, and curious learners who capture ideas from screenshots, links, and raw text, then turn them into structured notes, review items, and quizzes. They use it as a lightweight AI knowledge inbox that should feel calm and immediate, not overwhelming.

### Brand Personality
Clear, calm, quietly magical.
The product should evoke focus, trust, and a subtle sense of wonder without looking ornate, dark, or fantasy-heavy.

### Aesthetic Direction
Brutally minimal light-mode app UI with restrained magical accents. Primary references are Apple Notes, Bear, and Notion for clarity, whitespace, and hierarchy. Anti-references: heavy parchment textures, loud gold-on-black themes, excessive decorative illustration, over-designed dashboards.

### Design Principles
1. Content first: information hierarchy must be clearer than decoration.
2. Quiet magic: use gold only as a subtle accent, never as the main surface treatment.
3. Air and calm: generous whitespace, soft borders, and low-noise surfaces.
4. Fast capture to insight: upload and analysis flows should feel obvious and frictionless.
5. Minimal but warm: clean, contemporary UI with a slight magical signature.
